Album: LIVES OUTGROWN

By: Beth Gobbins


More about Beth Gobbins

Lives Outgrown is the debut solo studio album by English musician Beth Gibbons, released on 17 May 2024 through Domino Recording Company. The album was produced by Gibbons, James Ford and Lee Harris. It was preceded by the singles "Floating on a Moment", "Reaching Out" and "Lost Changes".
