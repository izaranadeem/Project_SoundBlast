ATIF ASLAM

INTRO:

Atif Aslam is a renowned Pakistani playback singer, songwriter, and actor known for his soulful voice and versatile singing style. Born on March 12, 1983, in Wazirabad, Punjab, Pakistan, Atif initially pursued a career in cricket before turning to music. He gained prominence as the lead vocalist of the Pakistani band Jal, with their debut single "Aadat" becoming a major hit in 2004.

Projects:

Coke Studio Pakistan: Atif Aslam has been a prominent figure in Coke Studio Pakistan, where he has collaborated with various artists and musicians to create unique renditions of traditional and contemporary songs. His performances like "Tajdar-e-Haram" and "Wohi Khuda Hai" are widely acclaimed.
Solo Albums: His solo albums "Jal Pari," "Doorie," and "Meri Kahani" feature collaborations with various musicians and have produced several hits.
