DUA LIPA

INTRO:

Dua Lipa is a British-Albanian singer, songwriter, and model who has rapidly risen to international fame since her debut. Born on August 22, 1995, in London, she began her music career at a young age, posting covers on YouTube. In 2015, she signed with Warner Music Group and released her first single, "New Love." Her self-titled debut album in 2017 included hits like "Be the One" and "New Rules," which topped charts worldwide. Known for her distinctive voice and disco-pop sound, Lipa has won several awards, including Grammys for Best New Artist and Best Pop Vocal Album for her second album, "Future Nostalgia." She is also noted for her fashion sense and has modeled for various high-profile brands.


COLLABORATIONS:

Dua Lipa has collaborated with various artists across different genres, showcasing her versatility and broad appeal. Some of her notable collaborations include:

"One Kiss" with Calvin Harris - A dance hit that topped charts worldwide and became one of the best-selling songs of 2018.
"No Lie" with Sean Paul - A reggae-influenced track that gained significant popularity and showcased her versatility
"Prisoner" with Miley Cyrus - A rock-influenced duet that featured on Cyrus's album "Plastic Hearts.
"Scared to Be Lonely" with Martin Garrix - A heartfelt EDM track that became a commercial success
"My Love" with Wale, Major Lazer, and WizKid - A collaboration that blends pop with Afrobeat and dancehall influences
"Sugar" with BROCKHAMPTON - A remix version where Dua Lipa's vocals added a new dimension to the song.

These collaborations highlight Dua Lipa's ability to seamlessly blend her style with various musical genres and artists.
