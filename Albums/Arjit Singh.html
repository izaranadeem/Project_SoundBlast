ARIJIT SINGH

INTRO:

Arijit Singh is a prominent Indian playback singer, renowned for his versatility and emotive voice. Born on April 25, 1987, in Jiaganj, Murshidabad, West Bengal, Arijit started his musical journey under the guidance of his mother, who is also a singer. He gained significant recognition after participating in the reality show "Fame Gurukul" in 2005, although he did not win. His major breakthrough came with the song "Tum Hi Ho" from the movie "Aashiqui 2" in 2013, which catapulted him to fame. Arijit's music spans various genres including romantic, soulful, and classical songs, and he has lent his voice to numerous hit Bollywood tracks. His ability to convey deep emotions through his singing has made him a favorite among music lovers. He has won several awards, including multiple Filmfare Awards and IIFA Awards, and is widely regarded as one of the leading playback singers in the Indian music industry. In addition to his singing career, Arijit Singh is also involved in music production and live performances. Despite his widespread fame, he is known for his humble and down-to-earth personality.

COLLABORATIONS:

Arijit and Shreya have delivered hits like "Samjhawan" from "Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania" and "Tera Fitoor" from "Genius."
Sunidhi Chauhan: Their collaborations include the popular song "Darkhaast" from "Shivaay."
Neha Kakkar: Arijit and Neha have collaborated on songs like "Khuda Bhi Jab" from "Ek Paheli Leela."
Palak Muchhal: They have sung together in songs such as "Chahun Main Ya Naa" from "Aashiqui 2."


International Collaborations:

The Vamps: Arijit collaborated with the British band The Vamps for a special version of their song "Beliya."
DJ Bravo: Arijit worked with the cricketer-turned-musician DJ Bravo on the song "Jager Bomb" from the movie "Tum Bin 2."


Albums and Projects

"Love Aaj Kal" (2020): Arijit worked extensively with composer Pritam, delivering hits like "Shayad" and "Haan Main Galat."
"Ae Dil Hai Mushkil" (2016): This album, composed by Pritam, features Arijit's chart-topping title track as well as other popular songs like "Channa Mereya."
These collaborations have played a significant role in shaping Arijit Singh's illustrious career, allowing him to explore different musical styles and reach a diverse audience.
