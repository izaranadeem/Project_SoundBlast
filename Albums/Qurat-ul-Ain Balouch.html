Qurat-ul-Ain Balouch

INTRO:

Qurat-ul-Ain Balouch, commonly known as QB, is a prominent Pakistani singer known for her soulful voice and captivating performances. She was born on March 4, 1988, in Aradan, Iran, but her family is of Pakistani descent, and she grew up in Pakistan. Balouch gained widespread recognition with her cover of "Akhiyan Nu Rehn De," which became a viral hit and established her as a notable figure in the Pakistani music industry. Balouch's major breakthrough came when she sang "Wohi Humsafar Tha" for the critically acclaimed Pakistani drama series "Humsafar." The song was a massive success and became synonymous with the show, earning her numerous awards and accolades. Her unique voice and emotional depth have made her a favorite among fans of contemporary Pakistani music.

COLLABORATIONS:

Qurat-ul-Ain Balouch (QB) has collaborated with several prominent artists and projects, showcasing her versatility and talent. Some of her notable collaborations include:
Coke Studio: QB has been a part of Coke Studio, Pakistan's renowned music television series that brings together diverse musical talent. Her collaborations on this platform have been particularly celebrated.
"Sammi Meri Waar": QB collaborated with Umair Jaswal in Season 8 of Coke Studio. The song became an instant hit and is remembered for its energetic performance and unique blend of traditional and contemporary styles.
"Laung Gawacha": In Season 10 of Coke Studio, she performed this classic Punjabi folk song, giving it a modern twist while preserving its traditional essence.
Hadiqa Kiani: QB joined forces with another iconic Pakistani singer, Hadiqa Kiani, for a rendition of "Janaan" on a musical platform. This collaboration was well-received and showcased the vocal prowess of both artists.
Noori: She collaborated with the popular Pakistani rock band Noori on the song "Paar Chanaa De" for Coke Studio Season 9. This fusion of rock and folk elements was widely appreciated and demonstrated QB's ability to cross genres seamlessly.
Ali Zafar: QB has worked with Ali Zafar, a renowned Pakistani singer and actor, on various musical projects. Their collaborations highlight the harmonious blend of their distinctive vocal styles.
Shaani Arshad: For the soundtrack of the Pakistani film "Superstar," QB sang "Bekaraan," composed by Shaani Arshad. The song received critical acclaim and added to the emotional depth of the film.
