BILLIE EILISH

INTRO:

Billie Eilish Pirate Baird O'Connell is an American singer and songwriter, born in December 18, 2001 (age 22 years), Los Angeles, California, United States. She first gained public attention in 2015 with her debut single "Ocean Eyes", written and produced by her brother Finneas O'Connell, with whom she collaborates on music and live shows. She is one of a few artist who came out in support of Palestine. Since releasing her viral hit 'Ocean Eyes' in 2015, Billie Eilish has become one of the biggest pop stars in the world following her debut album WHEN WE ALL FALL ASLEEP, WHERE DO WE GO?.


COLLABORATIONS:

Billie Eilish has collaborated with various artists and producers across different genres. Some notable collaborations include:
1.Bad Guy (with Justin Bieber) - A remix of her hit song featuring Justin Bieber.
2.Lovely (with Khalid) - A hauntingly beautiful track that showcases their combined talents.
3.Everything I Wanted (with Finneas) - A collaboration with her brother, Finneas O'Connell, who often collaborates with her as a producer.
4.No Time to Die - The theme song for the James Bond film of the same name, which she co-wrote with Finneas.
5.My Future - Another single produced in collaboration with Finneas.

These collaborations highlight her versatility and ability to create impactful music across different styles and themes.


AWARDS:

Billie Eilish has won numerous awards and accolades for her music and contributions to the music industry. Some of her notable awards include multiple Grammy Awards, American Music Awards, MTV Video Music Awards, Billboard Music Awards, and Brit Awards. She has been recognized for her distinctive sound, songwriting abilities, and unique style.

Billie won multiple Grammy Awards including Album of the Year, Record of the Year, Song of the Year, and Best New Artist in 2020.
She has also won multiple MTV Video Music Awards in categories such as Best New Artist, Best Editing, and Best Cinematography.


NET WORTH:

Billie Eilish's net worth is estimated to be around $25 to $30 million, which she earned through her album sales and tours.
